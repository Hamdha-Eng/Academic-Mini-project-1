About Smart Panagamuwa

Smart Panagamuwa is a visionary initiative aimed at transforming our hometown into a modern, efficient, and sustainable community by harnessing the power of technology. The goal is to solve local challenges and improve the quality of life through innovative, data-driven solutions in key sectors such as healthcare, waste management, transportation, and community engagement.
